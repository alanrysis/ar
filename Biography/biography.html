Biography

Alan Rysis is an artist driven by passion and creation!

Based on his influences and experience, Alan retains what is best done in the electronic music world and transports all these feelings and knowledge to his productions.

For Alan, the creation is the most important value that surround the world of electronic music, and the perfect production is the one which there is a perfect balance between technique and creativity.

Alan is an artist that is not satisfied with his knowledge of today, he is always looking for new ways, new forms of musical expression, but never forgeting his fingerprint, his way of conveying feelings or ideas through instruments, melodies and sound frequencies.

The Beginning

Alan was still a child when he began to explore his musicality by playing melodies in an organ that had been offered to him and his brothers.
Without great musical knowledge, that period served for Alan to train the ability to recognize notes and melodies and to play them only by ear.

A few years later, in his adolescence, and with electronic music in his luggage, it was possible to begin his journey in musical creation through simple computer applications that allowed him to raise his musical creativity to a level he had never before thought possible.
This feat was like a Big Bang in his creativity, leaving several fragments and many possibilities that still explores in his current studio.

The DJ

Alan knows how to mix, how to build a flowent mix, and how to re-adapt the musical journey to the crowd without any sync problem and without using the sync button. But with the evolution of time, he decided to focus all his time to the production side, alowing him to evolve better in the creations than in turntable mixes.

The Computer Engineer

As a almost graduated Computer Engineer, Alan has already created millions lines of code that are being rendered by web browsers every day.
